Getting Started

Welcome to Purple Pen!

If this is your first time using Purple Pen, here are the best ways to get help and learn the program.

Getting Help

There are two important ways to get quick help while using Purple Pen.

  • Check the quick help bar (or status bar) at the bottom of the Purple Pen window. When you are performing a task, this area will give you quick instructions.
  • When you are in a dialog box, click the question mark at the top-right corner of the window to get help about that command.
