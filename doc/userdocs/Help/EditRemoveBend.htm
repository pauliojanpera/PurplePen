Item/Remove Bend

Note: This command is available only when a leg with bends in it, or when a out-of-bounds area, dangerous area, or uncrossable boundary is selected.

If a leg has a bend in it (created with the Item/Add Bend command), this command is used to remove it. After selecting the Remove Bend command, click the mouse on the bend you would like to remove.

This command is also used to removes a corner from a special item such as a dangerous area or uncrossable boundary. After choosing the Remove Corner command, click the mouse on the corner you would like to remove. A dangerous area or out-of-bounds area must have at least three corners, so you cannot remove a corner from a special area that has only three corners.
