Add/Water Location

Adds a special item indicating the location of water or refreshments that are not located at a control point. Click the mouse at the location on the map of the refreshments.

If water is located at a control point, this special item should not be used; the water control symbol should be placed in the last column of the control description instead.

By default, the water location special item appears on all courses. If you wish it to appear only on some courses, use the Item/Change Displayed Courses command.
