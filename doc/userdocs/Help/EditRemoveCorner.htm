Edit/Remove Corner

Note: This command only appears when a out-of-bounds area, dangerous area, or uncrossable boundary is selected.

Removes a corner from a special item such as a dangerous area or uncrossable boundary. After choosing the Remove Corner command, click the mouse on the corner you would like to remove.

A dangerous area or out-of-bounds area must have at least three corners, so you cannot remove a corner from a special area that has only three corners.
