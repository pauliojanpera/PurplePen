Add/Dangerous Area

Draws an area that is dangerous for competitors.

Draw the area on the map with the mouse as follows:

  • Press and hold the left mouse button at the location where the one corner of the area should be.
  • Move the mouse to where the next corner should appear, and release the mouse button.
  • Press and hold the left mouse button again to draw the next side of the area.
  • Move the mouse to where the next corner should appear, and release the mouse button.
  • After releasing the mouse button at the last corner, click the mouse once to finish drawing.

By default, the dangerous area special item appears on all courses. If you wish it to appear only on some courses, use the Item/Change Displayed Courses command.
